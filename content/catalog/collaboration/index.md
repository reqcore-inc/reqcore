---
title: Collaboration
description: "Tools for hiring teams to work together — comments, activity logs, scheduling, and candidate communication."
---

Hiring is a team sport. Collaboration features let recruiters, hiring managers, and interviewers share context, leave notes, schedule interviews, and communicate with candidates — all from within Reqcore.

These features are planned for Phase 3 of the roadmap, after the AI intelligence layer is in place.
