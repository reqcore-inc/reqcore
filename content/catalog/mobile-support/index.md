Mobile support ensures the core hiring workflow works well on phones and tablets, not just desktop screens. Recruiters can quickly review candidates, move applications through stages, and stay responsive when they are away from their primary workstation.

This category focuses on responsive UX and practical in-the-field hiring operations.
